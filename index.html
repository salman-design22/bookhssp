About High Security Registration Plates

High Security Registration Plates (HSRP) are government-mandated registration plates crafted from high-quality aluminum, equipped with unique anti-tampering security parameters designed to minimize automobile-related fraud and crime.

🔒 Chromium Hologram

Hot-stamped chromium emblem featuring the Ashoka Chakra to prevent counterfeit replication.

🔢 Unique Laser Serial Number

An absolute unique 10-digit PIN laser engraved for tracking plate alignment with individual vehicle data across servers.

⚙ Snap Lock Seals

Uses non-reusable snap locks preventing any malicious removal of the registered plate.

🚗 Reflective Sheeting

Special retro-reflective sheets allowing heightened visibility under extreme night settings.

Official Government Notifications

Under Rule 50 of the Central Motor Vehicles Rules (CMVR), the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) has mandated the installation of High Security Registration Plates across all legacy and current vehicles.

MoRTH DIRECTIVE

Mandatory Compliance Order

Failure to mount HSRP on registered vehicles attracts penalties as declared by respective state transport departments.
